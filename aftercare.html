How to Care for a New Tattoo - Initial Cleaning and Care

    • Listen to your artists personal perfrences on how to heal their tattoos.
      Avoid contact with fresh tattoo for 2-3 hours.
      Gently clean your tattoo after 3 hours with warm water and soap
      Apply a thin layer of aquaphor on your skin
      Thin layers are important, too much application of lotion will not allow the tattoo to breathe.
      Every 3-5 hours for the next 2 weeks, clean your tattoo with a wet paper towel and soap is optional
      After cleaning, apply another application of aquapor.
